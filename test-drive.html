Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Tesla test drives.

What do I need to bring to my test drive? +
Please bring a valid driver's license. International licenses are accepted as long as they are valid in the United States.
Can I bring guests to my test drive? +
Yes, you may bring up to three guests. However, only licensed drivers will be permitted to drive the vehicle.
What happens if I need to reschedule? +
You can reschedule your test drive by contacting us at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment. You can do this by calling 1-888-518-3752 or through your Tesla account.
Are test drives available on weekends? +
Yes, test drives are available seven days a week. Weekend appointments tend to fill up quickly, so we recommend scheduling in advance.
Can I test drive multiple vehicles? +
Depending on availability and time constraints, you may be able to test drive multiple vehicles. Please discuss this with your Tesla advisor when you arrive.
